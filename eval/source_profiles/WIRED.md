# WIRED

House style:
- US spelling, conversational technology reporting.
- WIRED is all caps and reads as Wired.

Proper nouns:
- WIRED, AI Lab, Eka Robotics, DeepSeek, ChatGPT.
- Pulkit Agrawal, Tuomas Haarnoja, Google DeepMind, MIT.
- Alana Levinson, Jennifer Swann, D4vd, LAPD, Coachella.
- Joshua Tree, Vons, Celeste Rivas Hernandez, Kevin Kelly, Baxter.

Domain terms:
- AI reads as A I.
- LA reads as L A when referring to Los Angeles.
- LAPD reads as L A P D.
- D4vd reads as David when the artist is referenced.
- ChatGPT reads as Chat G P T.
- Open-world reads as open world.

Homograph cues:
- Robot demos and benchmarks use model in the machine-learning sense.
- Arms usually means robotic arms in the Eka story.

Boilerplate to strip:
- Newsletter framing such as "In this week's AI Lab".
- Cross-promo blurbs after the main article.
- Repeated social or subscription footer copy.
