# The Atlantic

House style:
- US spelling, polished magazine prose, formal but essayistic.
- Preserve long sentences, rhetorical questions, and quoted phrases.

Proper nouns:
- The Atlantic, Richard Dawkins, Claude, Anthropic, UnHerd.
- Alan Turing, Tom McClelland, Eric Schwitzgebel, UC Riverside.
- OpenAI, ChatGPT, Elon Musk, Sam Altman, xAI, Danny Hillis.
- IRS, Danny Werfel, Kaschit Pandya, Cleveland Clinic.

Domain terms:
- AI reads as A I.
- AIs reads as A Is.
- ChatGPT reads as Chat G P T.
- CFO reads as C F O. IRS reads as I R S.
- ARPANET reads as ARPA net.
- M.B.A. reads as M B A.
- IMF reads as I M F in IRS technology context.

Homograph cues:
- Consciousness terms should be read as philosophy terms, not clinical labels.
- Agent can mean AI agent unless the sentence is about a person or agency.

Boilerplate to strip:
- Subscription calls to action.
- Repeated related-article or newsletter blocks after the article body.
