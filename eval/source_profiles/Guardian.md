# Guardian

House style:
- UK spelling, lively cultural-news tone.
- Keep British idiom and publication names exactly.

Proper nouns:
- The Guardian, Graham Norton, Edinburgh fringe, Perrier award.
- Tracey Emin, Steven Soderbergh, Jerry Saltz, Michael Billington.
- Koons, Emin, Bowie, Greater Manchester.

Domain terms:
- AI reads as A I.
- 90s reads as nineties when referring to the decade.
- Primetime reads as prime time if it appears split in speech context.
- One-person show reads as one person show.

Homograph cues:
- Fringe reads as the Edinburgh arts festival when capitalised or near Edinburgh.
- Ranked reads as the Guardian feature title when capitalised.

Boilerplate to strip:
- "Join" event promotions.
- Cross-promo link cards and short recommendation blurbs after the main item.
- Newsletter footer and subscription prompts.
