# Bloomberg

House style:
- US spelling, business-news tone, formal but conversational.
- Preserve Money Stuff voice and quoted legal or finance terms.

Proper nouns:
- Matt Levine, Bloomberg, Money Stuff.
- JPMorganChase reads as J P Morgan Chase.
- BDCs reads as B D Cs unless expanded nearby as business development companies.

Domain terms:
- bps reads as basis points.
- bp reads as basis point only in finance-rate context.
- PE reads as P E or private equity when explicitly financial.
- NAV reads as N A V in fund accounting.
- EBITDA reads as E B I T D A.
- USD and dollar signs read as dollars only when attached to money amounts.

Homograph cues:
- Lead as a verb in "lead a deal" reads leed.
- Wound in finance context is usually woond only when injury is meant.

Boilerplate to strip:
- Newsletter sign-up prompts.
- Bloomberg subscription prompts.
- Repeated "Elsewhere" link blocks when detached from article prose.
